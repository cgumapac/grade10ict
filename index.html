Cavite National High School (Pambansang Mataas na Paaralan ng Cavite) formerly known as Cavite High School, is a Secondary School located at Chief E. Martin Street, Caridad, Cavite City. It was established in 1902 and it is considered as one of the oldest national secondary schools in the country. Cavite National High School’s 114 years of existence, transcendentally braved the changing times. Its remarkable role as trainer and molder of significant people and heroes effortlessly made a giant imprint in Philippine History. The school is guided by the principle-- Train and develop a child to be God-fearing, civic-oriented, highly responsible, participative, innovative, and above all a conscientious citizen of the country who shows concern for the preservation and conservation of nature and life itself.
